## ISSUE 1

The intended workflow for General Documents uploads is not correctly implemented.

---

## REQUIRED WORKFLOW

General Information
└── General Documents
└── Subfolders

All authenticated members (except Visitors) may upload documents to:

* General Documents
* Any subfolder beneath General Documents

This includes:

* Member
* Secretary
* Treasurer
* Junior Warden
* Senior Warden
* Worshipful Master
* Past Master
* Site Administrator
* PM Super Administrator

Visitors may not upload.

---

## PENDING STATUS

When a document is uploaded by a user:

Status = Pending

The document is NOT yet considered published.

Pending documents:

Visible to:

* uploader
* Site Administrator
* PM Super Administrator

Not visible to other members.

---

## UPLOADER RIGHTS

While status = Pending

Uploader may:

* delete own pending document
* replace own pending document if feasible
* edit description if feasible

Uploader may NOT:

* approve
* publish
* move to another folder
* change access rules

---

## APPROVAL RIGHTS

Only:

* Site Administrator
* PM Super Administrator

may:

* Approve
* Reject
* Archive
* Delete

pending documents.

---

## PUBLISHED STATUS

When approved:

Status = Published

Published document becomes visible according to folder permissions.

Once Published:

Uploader may no longer delete the document.

Published document may only be:

* Archived
* Deleted

by:

* Site Administrator
* PM Super Administrator

---

## ISSUE 2

Currently uploading to subfolders beneath:

General Information
└── General Documents

results in:

"Access Denied"

even for administrators.

---

## REQUIRED FIX

Subfolders must inherit upload permissions from the parent folder.

Example:

General Documents
├── 2026
├── Lodge Guidelines
└── Historical Articles

If a user may upload to General Documents,

then the same user may upload to:

* 2026
* Lodge Guidelines
* Historical Articles

unless explicit restrictions are implemented later.

---

## SUBFOLDER INHERITANCE

Default rule:

Parent folder permissions are inherited by all subfolders.

For this sprint:

No separate upload permissions at subfolder level.

Do not require manual permission assignment for each subfolder.

## AUDIT LOGGING

Log:

Peter Jones archived document "Guidelines.pdf".

---

## ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

1. Members can upload to General Documents.
2. Members can upload to subfolders under General Documents.
3. Administrators can upload to subfolders under General Documents.
4. Uploaded files receive Pending status.
5. Uploader may delete own Pending file.
6. Uploader cannot delete Published file.
7. Only Site Administrator and PM Super Administrator may approve.
8. Only Site Administrator and PM Super Administrator may archive/delete Published files.
9. Subfolders inherit upload permissions from General Documents.
10. No Access Denied error occurs for valid uploads.
11. Build passes.
