--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 — MEMBER TIMELINE
--------------------------------------------------

Add a Member Timeline section at the bottom of the Member Detail page.

Purpose:

Provide a complete chronological history of important member lifecycle events.

Display newest first.

Examples:

12 Jan 2026
Member created by John Smith

12 Jan 2026
Invitation created

12 Jan 2026
Invitation sent

15 Jan 2026
Invitation accepted

15 Jan 2026
First login

22 Feb 2026
Degree changed to Fellowcraft

01 Apr 2026
Role Treasurer granted

14 Jun 2026
Password reset by Site Administrator

20 Jun 2026
Passkey registered

--------------------------------------------------
TIMELINE EVENTS
--------------------------------------------------

Include whenever available:

Account Events
- Member created
- Member activated
- Member deactivated
- Member deleted

Invitation Events
- Invitation created
- Invitation sent
- Invitation resent
- Invitation accepted
- Invitation expired
- Invitation revoked

Authentication Events
- First login
- Last login
- Password reset
- Password changed
- Passkey added
- Passkey removed
- 2FA enabled
- 2FA disabled

Role Events
- Role granted
- Role revoked

Degree Events
- Degree granted
- Degree revoked

Membership Events
- Membership status changed

--------------------------------------------------
UI REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------

Display:

- Date and time
- Event description
- User who performed the action (if applicable)

Example:

21 Jun 2026 19:42

Role Treasurer granted by Peter Jones

Allow scrolling for long histories.

Mobile friendly.

--------------------------------------------------
AUDIT LOG INTEGRATION
--------------------------------------------------

Where possible, derive timeline information from existing audit logs.

Avoid creating duplicate storage if the information already exists.

--------------------------------------------------
ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
--------------------------------------------------

1. Member timeline is visible on the Member Detail page.
2. Timeline shows key lifecycle events.
3. Timeline is ordered chronologically.
4. Timeline remains readable on mobile devices.
5. Timeline automatically updates when new events occur.
6. Build passes.
