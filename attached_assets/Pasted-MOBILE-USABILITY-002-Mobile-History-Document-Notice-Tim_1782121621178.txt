ITEM 1 — HISTORY MOBILE NAVIGATION
--------------------------------------------------

Issue:
On iPhone/mobile, the History section navigation goes beyond the screen width, especially around the General John J. Pershing section.

Required behavior:

Desktop:
- Horizontal tabs may remain.

Mobile/tablet:
- Convert History sub-navigation to vertical stacked menu, accordion, or dropdown.
- No horizontal scrolling.
- No content wider than the viewport.
- General John J. Pershing section must be fully readable on iPhone.

Acceptance:
- History page fits within phone width.
- All History sections remain reachable.
- No horizontal overflow.

--------------------------------------------------
ITEM 2 — DOCUMENT DISCLAIMER ACCEPTANCE TIMESTAMP
--------------------------------------------------

Issue:
After a member accepts the Document Library disclaimer, it is not clearly shown when they accepted it.

Required behavior:

Store and show:
- acceptedAt date/time
- disclaimerVersion

Collapsed view:
Show:

✓ Accepted on 22 Jun 2026 at 20:47

Expanded view:
At the bottom of the disclaimer, show:

You accepted these rules on:
22 Jun 2026 at 20:47

Future-proof:
- Keep disclaimer version support.
- If disclaimer version changes later, users can be asked to accept again.

Acceptance:
- Acceptance timestamp visible after acceptance.
- Timestamp remains visible after logout/login.
- Works on mobile.

--------------------------------------------------
ITEM 3 — MEMBERS LIST CLICKABILITY
--------------------------------------------------

Issue:
In Management → Members, the member list does not clearly show that a member row/name can be clicked to open the Member Detail page.

Required behavior:

Make member rows clearly actionable.

Options:
- Add an explicit button: View / Manage
- Make the full row hover/clickable
- Add cursor pointer on hover
- Add visual affordance, e.g. chevron arrow →
- Add tooltip: Open member details

Recommended:

Add a clear action button:

View / Manage

at the right side of each member row.

Also make the member name clickable.

Mobile:
- Button should remain visible and easy to tap.
- Avoid tiny icons only.

Acceptance:
- It is obvious how to open member details.
- Row/name click works.
- View / Manage button works.
- Mobile users can easily tap it.
